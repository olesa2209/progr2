Chamomile is an absolutely unpretentious plant that blooms in the first year of life. It has a thin stem and narrow green leaves. The flowers consist of a bright yellow core and elongated snow-white petals. They are collected in baskets in small groups, so that the flowering seems lush. Chamomile grows up to 60 cm in height. It grows on any soil, tolerates drought and other adverse factors. It is grown both on an industrial scale and as a decoration of house flower beds. Chamomile can be planted with seedlings and seeds.
